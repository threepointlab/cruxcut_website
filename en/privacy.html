Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

CruxCut processes and manages personal information lawfully and securely in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act and related laws and regulations to protect the freedom and rights of data subjects. In accordance with Article 30 of the Personal Information Protection Act, this policy explains the procedures and standards for processing and protecting personal information and provides a way to handle related complaints promptly and smoothly.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

CruxCut processes personal information for the following purposes. Personal information being processed will not be used for any purpose other than the following, and if the purpose changes, necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent will be taken in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Membership Registration and Management

We process personal information for confirming membership intent, user identification and authentication, maintaining and managing membership status, preventing misuse of services, sending notices and announcements, and handling complaints.

2. Service Provision

AI climbing movement analysis

We analyze climbing videos and images uploaded by users to provide movement feedback, route records, and analysis results. Cloud storage and synchronization are provided to users who purchase a subscription plan, and analysis data for general users is processed only on-device and is not collected on the server.

2. Personal Information Collected

CruxCut collects and uses the following personal information.

Personal information processed without consent

1. Simple login

  • Collected item: Email
  • Purpose: Used to identify the user through the email used for simple login.

2. Service provision

  • Collected item: climbing videos and images uploaded by the user, movement analysis results, route/session records, label information, service data change logs
  • Purpose: Providing service features for logged-in users

3. Automatically collected information

  • Collected item: service usage records, device information (OS version, device model, app version), app crash logs
  • Purpose: Service stability, error diagnosis, and feature improvement

3. Retention and Use Period

CruxCut processes and retains personal information within the retention and use period prescribed by law or agreed to by the data subject when collecting the information.

  • All related personal information is destroyed when a user deletes their account.
  • Service data change logs are retained for 90 days from the time of storage and then destroyed.

4. Destruction Procedures and Methods

CruxCut destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary due to the expiration of the retention period or achievement of the processing purpose.

If personal information must continue to be retained under other laws even after the agreed retention period has expired or the processing purpose has been achieved, the information is moved to a separate database or stored in a different location.

Destruction procedure

CruxCut selects personal information for destruction when a destruction reason occurs and destroys it after approval from the privacy officer.

Destruction method

Electronic files containing personal information are destroyed so they cannot be restored.

6. Security Measures

CruxCut takes the following measures to secure personal information.

  • Access control and privilege management for personal information
  • Encryption of personal information (including encryption in transit)
